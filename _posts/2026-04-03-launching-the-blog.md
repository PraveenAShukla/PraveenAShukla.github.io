I am using this blog as a working notebook for ideas that sit between research and engineering.

Most posts here will be short, concrete, and technical. I plan to write about:

- graph deep learning experiments
- LLM agents and tool-use workflows
- healthcare AI prototypes
- evaluation, reproducibility, and failure analysis
- lessons from building fast and shipping cleanly

Some posts will be polished write-ups. Others will be brief notes from ongoing work, ablations, or implementation details that are useful to document while the context is fresh.

The goal is simple: think clearly, build rigorously, and leave behind artifacts that are actually useful.
